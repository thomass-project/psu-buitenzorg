Our History

Excellence in
Higher Education

University of Buitenzorg is a prestigious institution located in the historic city of Bogor. Founded in the colonial era, we have evolved into a world-class center for research and learning, blending heritage with modern innovation.

The university offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs across diverse disciplines, fostering an environment where critical thinking and creativity thrive. Our commitment is to produce graduates who are not only academically proficient but also ethically grounded.
