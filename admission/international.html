Admission Procedure

International applicants follow a slightly modified admission track. Ensure you have your passport details ready before starting the application.

1

Account Registration

Create a new account on the Student Dashboard using a valid international email.

2

Fill the Admission Form

Make sure you upload high-resolution scans of transcripts, passport, and English proficiency test.

3

Application Fee Payment

Pay the non-refundable application fee (PAS 20) via international transfer.

4

Interview

Online interview for selected majors (via Zoom/Meet) to assess academic readiness.

5

Letter of Acceptance (LoA)

Successful candidates receive an unconditional LoA required for visa application.

6

Visa Processing

University sponsors your Study Permit application.

Visas & Immigration

All international students require a Study Permit to reside in Pasundan. The University acts as your sponsor.

Required Documents for Visa Sponsorship:

  • Valid Passport (min. 18 months)
  • Letter of Acceptance (LoA)
  • Proof of Financial Solvency (PAS 5000+)
  • Health Insurance Policy
  • 4x6 White Background Photos
  • Statement of Law Compliance

Pre-Arrival Guide

  • Airport Pickup

    International students are prioritized for the "PSUB Dormitory" on-campus housing. Ensure you book 1 month in advance.

Living in Bogor

The Rain City

Bogor is famous for its daily afternoon rain. Always carry an umbrella or raincoat. The climate is cooler than Jakarta.

Local Customs

Sundanese people are polite. Smile often. Dress modestly on campus (no shorts/tank tops). Use your right hand to give/receive.

Laws & Safety

Strict drug laws apply in Pasundan. Carry a copy of your passport/KITAS at all times.
