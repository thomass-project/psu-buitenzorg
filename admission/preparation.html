Application Guide

Document Checklist

All documents must be scanned in high resolution PDF format.

1. Online Application Form

Must be filled out in English. Ensure your name matches your passport exactly.

2. Motivation Letter

A distinct letter for each study program applied for.

  • Format: Times New Roman, 12pt size.
  • Length: Minimum 1 page, maximum 2 pages.
  • Content: Why you chose this program, your future goals, and why Univ. of Buitenzorg.

3. Proof of Language Proficiency

Scan of original certificate.

TOEFL iBT > 80 IELTS > 6.0

4. School Certificates & Translations

Scanned copy of your original High School Diploma / Bachelor's Diploma.

Must include a sworn translation into English or Sundanese if the original is in another language.

5. Transcript of Records

List of all subjects taken and grades received.

6. Medical Certificate

Must be issued by a physician within the last 3 months.

  • Negative status for: HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C.
  • Chest X-ray report (Tuberculosis screening).
  • Proof of Yellow Fever vaccination (for applicants from risk zones).

7. Identification Document

Scan of a valid Passport (must be valid for at least 18 months from the start of the study program).
