Available Scholarships

University of Buitenzorg is committed to supporting students from diverse backgrounds. Please review the categories below carefully. Applications are processed on a rolling basis.

Scholarships for Domestic Students

Government

Federal Scholarship

Full tuition coverage mandated by the Department of Education for students showing exceptional national potential.

  • 100% Tuition Fee
  • Transport Allowance
  • Dormitory Access
  • Research Grant
  • Meal Plan Support
  • Monthly Stipend up to PAS 2,200
Institutional

University Scholarship

Awarded to high-achieving students entering specific strategic faculties within the university.

  • 75% Tuition Fee
  • Dormitory Access
  • Meal Plan Support
  • Transport Allowance
  • Monthly Stipend up to PAS 1,500
Regional

Pajajaran Scholarship

Exclusive for residents of Bogor, Sukabumi & Cianjur Residency. Proof of residency (ID Card) required.

  • 50% Tuition Fee
  • Transport Allowance
Merit Based

Academic Excellence

For applicants with an average High School score > 90.00 or equivalent distinctions.

  • Partial Funding
  • Book Allowance
Need Based

Pasundan Aid Grant

Assistance for students demonstrating significant financial need. Requires detailed financial disclosure.

  • Tuition Relief
  • Meal Plan Support

Scholarships for International Students

Diplomatic

International PSUB Scholarship

Bilateral agreement scholarships for students from friendly nations.

  • 100% Tuition Free
  • Dormitory Access
  • Visa & Flight Cost
  • Transport Allowance
  • Monthly Stipend up to PAS 2,200
Corporate

Industry Partnership

Sponsored by multinational partners for Engineering & Technology programs.

  • Tuition + Internship
  • Research Grant
Merit

Global Talent Award

For international students demonstrating outstanding academic or non-academic achievements.

  • Partial Tuition
  • Language Course
